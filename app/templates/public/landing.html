How it works

When your team cannot answer, LeadCare AI keeps the conversation going by text so leads do not slip away.

  1. Missed call detected

    An inbound call goes unanswered on your business line.

  2. Instant SMS follow-up

    The caller receives a friendly text asking what they need help with.

  3. Team alerted with details

    Replies are captured as a structured lead and your team is notified to call back.

Why texting works

  • Most callers won't leave a voicemail — but they will reply to a text.
  • You collect issue, location, and urgency before your team calls back.
  • Every missed call becomes a trackable lead instead of a dead end.
