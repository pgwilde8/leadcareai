Contractors Miss Calls for Real Reasons

Missed calls do not happen because contractors are careless. They happen because contractors are working.

  • On a ladder, in a crawl space, under a sink, or inside an attic
  • Driving between jobs, on another call, or meeting with a customer
  • Picking up supplies, eating lunch, or with family
  • Relying on your spouse or office helper to catch what they can

A customer with a leak, broken HVAC, damaged roof, or urgent repair may call the next contractor if nobody responds quickly. That is how missed calls become lost jobs.
