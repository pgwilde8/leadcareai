{# Reusable 4-step missed-call flow diagram (Phase C). #}

How {{ settings.app_name }} catches the calls that slip through

When a call is missed, {{ settings.app_name }} starts the conversation by text, collects the details, and alerts your team.

  1. Missed call

    Busy, unanswered, or after-hours — the caller does not reach a person.

  2. Instant text-back

    A friendly SMS goes out while the customer is still engaged.

  3. Job details captured

    Issue, location, and urgency are collected in a simple text thread.

  4. Owner/team alerted

    Your crew gets SMS or email with a clean lead summary to call back faster.
