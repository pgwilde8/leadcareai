How it works

Your business number still rings like normal. When nobody answers, {{ settings.app_name }} Backup Mode catches the lead.

  1. Missed, busy, or unanswered call

    Your phone provider forwards the call to your {{ settings.app_name }} recovery number (we guide setup and run a live test).

  2. Instant text to the homeowner

    The caller gets a friendly SMS asking about the roof issue, location, and urgency — while they’re still interested.

  3. Lead saved and your team alerted

    Replies become a clean lead summary in a simple dashboard so you can call back faster.
