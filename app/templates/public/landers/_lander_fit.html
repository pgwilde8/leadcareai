Who this is for — and who it is not

Good fit

  • Small service businesses that are mobile-first
  • Owners losing jobs to missed calls and slow voicemail
  • Family or staff backup when nobody can grab the phone
  • Teams that want text-back and a simple lead list — not a heavy CRM

Not ideal

  • Replacing a full live receptionist or 24/7 call center on every ring
  • 911, emergency dispatch, or life-threatening call routing
  • Complex PBX, custom IVR, or enterprise call-center requirements
