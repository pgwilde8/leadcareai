The missed-call problem

When your team is on a job, at lunch, or closed for the day, unanswered calls often mean lost revenue. Many callers will not leave a voicemail — they simply hire the next company that responds.

How {{ settings.app_name }} works

  1. Missed call — A customer calls and no one answers in time.
  2. Instant SMS — {{ settings.app_name }} sends a friendly text asking what they need.
  3. Qualification — Simple SMS questions capture name, need, and urgency.
  4. Team alert — Your staff gets an SMS, email, or dashboard alert with the lead details.

Keep your number — add smart follow-up

Many businesses keep their existing phone number and use call forwarding (missed or unanswered calls forwarded to {{ settings.app_name }}) so callers still see your brand. Setup varies by carrier; we help you configure it during onboarding.

{{ settings.app_name }} is not emergency dispatch and does not replace every traditional answering service, but it helps you recover more missed-call opportunities with fast SMS follow-up.
