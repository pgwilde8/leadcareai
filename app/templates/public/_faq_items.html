What happens when someone calls and we miss it?
LeadCare AI detects the missed call and sends an immediate SMS to the caller asking what they need help with. Replies are collected as structured lead details and your team is notified.
Do we need to change our phone number?
In most setups, calls forward to your existing number or a dedicated LeadCare line. We help you configure forwarding during onboarding so your team keeps familiar workflows.
Is this compliant with SMS regulations?
Yes — we support opt-out language, consent capture, and business-specific SMS terms. Your onboarding includes compliance review for your use case.
How long does setup take?
Most businesses are live within a few business days after signup. One-time setup covers configuration, messaging templates, and team notification preferences.
Can I try it before signing up?
Yes. Call our live demo number, let it go unanswered, reply to the text, and view your masked lead on the public demo dashboard.
