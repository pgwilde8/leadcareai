Objection answers

Do I have to change my number?
No. Most businesses keep their existing customer-facing number and forward missed or unanswered calls to a {{ settings.app_name }} recovery line. We help with forwarding setup during onboarding.
Is this an answering service?
It is missed-call recovery by SMS and lead qualification — not a live call center answering every ring. It helps you respond faster to people who already tried to reach you.
What happens if I cancel?
They can cancel per the customer terms. Forwarding can be removed with their carrier. Point them to Terms and Refund policy for specifics; do not promise custom terms.
Does AI answer the phone?
{{ settings.app_name }} focuses on text follow-up after a missed call, not replacing a human who picks up live calls. Staff still call the customer back; the system captures and qualifies the lead first.
What industries is this for?
Local service businesses that live on the phone — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, restoration, landscaping, cleaning, and similar trades where a missed call often means a lost job.
