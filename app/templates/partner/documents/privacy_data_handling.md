# Privacy and Data Handling Agreement

Partners who access LeadCareAI systems or customer-related information must protect data.

## Confidentiality

- Do not disclose non-public Company information, customer data, or platform credentials.
- Do not sell, scrape, or misuse lead or customer information.

## Access

- Use only accounts and data you are authorized to access.
- Do not share login credentials.
- Report suspected unauthorized access promptly.

## Customer contact

- Do not contact customer leads except as authorized by LeadCareAI and applicable law.
- Do not export or publish screenshots containing personal information without approval.

## Security

Company may suspend partner access if misuse or security risk is suspected.

## After termination

Return or delete confidential materials upon request and stop using Company systems and brand assets.
