# Acceptable Marketing Policy

Partners must represent LeadCareAI honestly and professionally.

## You may

- Share approved demo links, referral links, and sales scripts.
- Explain missed-call text-back, SMS lead intake, and dashboard follow-up in general terms.
- Book demos and help prospects understand next steps.

## You may not

- Guarantee income, leads, appointments, revenue, or business results.
- Claim passive income with no work, or guaranteed partner earnings.
- State that LeadCareAI is an emergency service, full answering service, or licensed professional service unless expressly approved in writing.
- Promise pricing, features, refunds, or custom terms not approved by LeadCareAI.
- Use spam, unlawful telemarketing, or deceptive lead generation.
- Impersonate LeadCareAI employees or bind the Company without written authority.

## Earnings claims

Any statement about how much partners can earn must match written Company materials only.

## Compliance

You are responsible for complying with applicable advertising, SMS, email, privacy, and consumer protection laws.
