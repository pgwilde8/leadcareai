Simple summary

{{ settings.app_name }} Sales Partners earn commissions by referring real business customers who sign up, pay, and remain active. You are paid for helping local businesses use {{ settings.app_name }} — not for recruiting people.

{{ settings.app_name }} helps businesses respond faster to missed calls, qualify leads by SMS, and alert their team before hot prospects go cold.

What customers buy

The standard {{ settings.app_name }} Growth Plan is:

Item Customer price
Setup fee $199 one-time
Monthly subscription $147/month

The setup fee helps cover onboarding, phone/SMS setup, business configuration, and support. The monthly subscription keeps the service active.

Your Sales Partner pay

As an approved {{ settings.app_name }} Sales Partner, you can earn:

Commission type Amount
Activation bonus $100 per activated paid customer
Monthly recurring commission $25/month per active paid customer
Retention bonus $100 after the customer stays paid for 6 months

Example: one customer

If you refer one business customer and they sign up for the Growth Plan:

Customer pays: $199 setup fee $147 first month

Your potential commission:

$100 activation bonus $25 monthly recurring commission $100 retention bonus after 6 paid months

If the customer remains active for 6 months, that one customer can be worth:

$100 activation bonus + $25/month for 6 months + $100 retention bonus = $350 total potential commission

After 6 months, you may continue earning $25/month while the customer remains active and eligible under the commission rules.

Example: 10 active customers

10 customers × $25/month = $250/month recurring commission

That is before any new activation bonuses or retention bonuses.

Example: 20 active customers

20 customers × $25/month = $500/month recurring commission

That is recurring commission while those customers remain active and paid.

When you earn

Commissions are based on real customer payments. A commission may be created when:

  1. The business customer signs up.
  2. The customer pays successfully.
  3. The customer is connected to your referral code/link or assigned to you by {{ settings.app_name }}.
  4. The customer becomes active.
  5. You are an approved and active Sales Partner.

Pending and approved commissions

Commissions are not paid instantly. When a customer pays, your commission is first marked as pending.

After the review period, the commission may become approved if:

  • The customer payment was successful.
  • The customer did not refund.
  • The customer did not dispute or charge back payment.
  • The customer is valid and active.
  • There is no fraud, fake account, or policy issue.
  • You followed {{ settings.app_name }} sales and marketing rules.

Approved commissions are paid on the company payout schedule.

Review period

{{ settings.app_name }} uses a review period before commissions become payable. This protects the company and partners from refunds, chargebacks, fake accounts, and cancelled customers.

Standard review flow:

  1. Customer pays
  2. Commission becomes pending
  3. Review period passes
  4. Commission becomes approved
  5. Approved commissions are paid

When commissions are not paid

No commission is paid for:

  • Free trials
  • Demo accounts
  • Test accounts
  • Fake accounts
  • Internal accounts
  • Unpaid customers
  • Cancelled customers
  • Refunded payments
  • Disputed payments
  • Chargebacks
  • Customers who never complete payment
  • Recruiting other partners by itself
  • Customers not properly connected to your referral code/link
  • Customers obtained through false, misleading, or unauthorized claims

Customer cancellation

Customers may cancel their subscription. If a customer cancels:

  • Future monthly recurring commissions stop.
  • Pending commissions may be cancelled.
  • Approved commissions may be reversed if payment is refunded, disputed, or charged back.
  • Paid commissions may be offset from future commissions if a refund, dispute, chargeback, or fraud issue occurs.

See also our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Retention bonus

You may earn a $100 retention bonus when a customer remains paid and active for 6 months. The retention bonus is not guaranteed at signup. The customer must remain valid, active, paid, and not refunded or disputed.

Recurring commission

You may earn $25/month for each active paid customer assigned to you. Recurring commission continues only while:

  • The customer remains active.
  • The customer continues paying.
  • The account is valid.
  • You remain eligible under the Sales Partner Agreement.
  • There are no refund, dispute, fraud, or policy issues.

What you should not say

Do not claim:

  • Guaranteed income
  • Passive income with no work
  • Guaranteed business results
  • Guaranteed leads
  • Guaranteed appointments
  • Guaranteed revenue
  • That {{ settings.app_name }} is an emergency service
  • That {{ settings.app_name }} is a full human answering service
  • That you are an employee of {{ settings.app_name }}
  • That customers can get features or pricing not approved by {{ settings.app_name }}

Do not make promises that are not in approved {{ settings.app_name }} materials.

Approved income language

You may say:
