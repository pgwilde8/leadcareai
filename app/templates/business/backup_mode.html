What is Backup Mode?

Backup Mode is your missed-call safety net. When call forwarding is enabled on your mobile line, missed, busy, or unreachable calls go to LeadCareAI. LeadCareAI texts callers, captures job details, saves the lead, and alerts your team.

  • Turn Backup Mode on when you are busy, after hours, driving, on a job, or need a break.
  • Turn forwarding off when you want calls going directly to you again.
  • Forwarding is controlled by your carrier. LeadCareAI guides you but cannot flip the carrier switch automatically.

When to use Backup Mode

  • On a job
  • Driving
  • Dinner or family time
  • After hours
  • Weekends
  • When your spouse or helper is busy
  • Vacation
  • Already on another call
