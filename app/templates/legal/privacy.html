Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

{{ settings.app_name }} (“we,” “us,” or “our”) provides software that helps local service businesses follow up on missed calls and manage leads by SMS. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website, use our platform, or interact with SMS programs operated through our service.

1. Who this policy applies to

  • Business customers who subscribe to {{ settings.app_name }}.
  • Business staff who log in to manage leads and messages.
  • End users (consumers) who call or text a business phone number connected to our service.
  • Website visitors who browse our marketing site or contact us.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Account and business information — name, email, business name, phone numbers, billing details, and settings you provide.
  • Lead and message data — phone numbers, message content, call/SMS metadata, lead status, notes, and timestamps when businesses use our platform.
  • Communications — support requests and correspondence with us.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and logs needed to operate and secure the service.

3. SMS and telecommunications data

When a business uses {{ settings.app_name }} for missed-call recovery or lead follow-up, we process SMS and related telephony data through providers such as Twilio. This may include the phone numbers involved, message body, delivery status, and carrier metadata. Businesses are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from their customers before sending marketing or automated texts, as described in our SMS Program Terms.

4. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the platform;
  • Route inbound and outbound SMS, create leads, and send notifications to business staff;
  • Process subscriptions and payments;
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and security issues;
  • Comply with law and enforce our agreements.

5. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the platform (for example, hosting, payment processing, SMS delivery, and email), subject to contractual safeguards. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Businesses may request deletion of certain data subject to applicable law and technical constraints.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your choices

  • Consumers can opt out of SMS from a business by replying STOP to that business’s messages.
  • Business users may access, update, or delete certain account information through the platform or by contacting us.
  • You may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws depending on your location.

9. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do. Continued use of the service after changes means you accept the updated policy.
