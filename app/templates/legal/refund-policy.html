2. Setup fee

The setup fee covers onboarding and account setup work, which may include:

  • Creating or activating your {{ settings.app_name }} account
  • Assigning or configuring a recovery/tracking phone number
  • Configuring missed-call text-back
  • Configuring SMS lead intake

3. Setup fee refunds

The setup fee is non-refundable once setup work has begun.

Setup work may begin after purchase and may include account creation, configuration, phone number assignment, SMS setup, onboarding, support, or testing.

If you request cancellation before any setup work has started, Webwise Solutions LLC may, at its sole discretion, issue a full or partial refund of the setup fee.

{{ settings.app_name }} is billed as a recurring monthly subscription unless otherwise stated in writing. Monthly subscription fees are billed in advance for the upcoming billing period. By subscribing, you authorize recurring billing until you cancel.

5. Monthly subscription refunds

Monthly subscription fees are non-refundable after billing, except where required by law or approved by Webwise Solutions LLC in its sole discretion.

We do not provide prorated refunds or credits for partial months, unused time, missed usage, or failure to use the service.

If you cancel during a billing period, your service may remain active until the end of the paid billing period unless your account is suspended or terminated for non-payment, misuse, legal risk, or violation of our Terms of Service.

You may cancel your subscription at any time before your next billing date. Cancellation stops future billing; it does not automatically refund fees already paid.
