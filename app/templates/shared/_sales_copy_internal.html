Sales copy (internal)

30-second demo script

“Try calling our demo line and don’t answer — you’ll get a text in seconds asking what you need. Reply like you’re a homeowner with a real problem. Watch the lead show up with urgency and details your team can act on. That’s what happens every time you miss a call after we’re live — without you changing your main phone number.”

Objection handlers

“Do I have to change my number?”
No. You keep the number customers already know. Unanswered or missed calls forward to a LeadCare recovery line we set up; SMS follow-up and lead capture run on that path.
“Is this an answering service?”
No. We don’t answer live calls for you. We recover missed calls with instant text-back, structured intake, and alerts to your team — so you call back with context, not a blank voicemail.
“What happens after hours?”
Same flow: missed call triggers SMS, we collect issue/location/urgency, and your team gets notified. You decide who follows up and when — the lead is waiting in the dashboard instead of lost.

Partner compensation reminder

Partners earn on paying customers they refer — not on recruiting other partners. V1 ledger (paid Stripe invoices only): $100 activation bonus, $25/month residual per paid month, $100 retention after 6 paid months. Payouts are tracked in admin; no automated payouts in V1.
